      Symbols

      SCATTER

      A Korean comic-style white tiger guardian. Scatter on reels 1 and 2 plus at least one scatter on reels 3, 4, or 5 triggers the bonus.

      WILD

      Substitutes for regular paying symbols and increases volatility.

      GAT

      A neon Korean gat symbol. It is the highest base-game award when connected across 5 reels.

      LANTERN

      A traditional cheongsachorong lantern in blue and red silk. Pays from 3 connected reels and anchors premium ways wins.

      SAMTAEGEUK

      A three-color Korean samtaegeuk charm. Pays from 3 connected reels as a low symbol.

      가 나 다

      Active Hangul rank symbols for the original stage cabinet theme. They pay from 3 connected reels.

      Bonus Features

      Dokkaebi Free Games

      The first dokkaebi gathers gems and bursts them into 10 free games. Bonus scatters may retrigger additional free games.

      4x3Dokkaebi Scratch

      The second dokkaebi bursts gems into a 4x3 scratch board. Match 3 MINI, MINOR, MAJOR, or GRAND symbols to collect the jackpot.

      Dokkaebi Expand

      The third dokkaebi bursts gems to expand reels 2-4 randomly from 5 to 10 rows for 5 bonus games.
